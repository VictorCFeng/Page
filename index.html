Eric & Wendy Schmidt Science Postdoctoral Fellow · Cornell University

My research spans energy and network systems, with a focus on cyber-physical systems across control, markets, and strategy. I am the Eric & Wendy Schmidt Science Postdoctoral Fellow at Cornell University, working with Prof. Fengqi You at the PEESE Lab.

I work toward a future of secure, sustainable energy. My current focus is the reliability, affordability and flexibility of energy systems under the continued growth of AI.

Previously: Ph.D. from Tsinghua University (2024); visiting scholar at the Automatic Control Lab, ETH Zürich (2023); B.E. from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (2019). Find my publications on Google Scholar and ResearchGate.
