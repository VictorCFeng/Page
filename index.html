Cheng Feng

 

I am currently Eric and Wendy Schmidt Science Postdoctoral Fellow, College of Engineering, Cornell University, working with Prof. Fengqi You.

I received the B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology in June 2019, and the Ph.D. degree in Electrical Engineering from Tsinghua University in June 2024. During February 2023 to August 2023, I was a visiting scholar in the Automatic Control Laboratory, ETH Zurich. Here is my CV

The publications of mine can be easily found on Google scholar and ResearchGate. Welcome discussions about my research!

Research

 

Grid Flexibility & Resilience: Investigates the synergy between communication and power networks to enhance grid flexibility and resilience through advanced optimization and control strategies.

Power Electronics & New Loads Explores the stability challenges and market mechanisms associated with the integration of inverter-based resources and high-demand loads, such as data centers.

Energy Digitization Drives the digital transformation of energy systems by leveraging digitization tools for smart industrial applications.
